Project Overview

At Sub800, we curate exceptional workspaces tailored to embody the essence of any business. Sub800 is the trusted partner of hundreds of industry-leading businesses seeking their perfect office space.

Sub800 brings unrivalled exposure to vacant workspaces, connecting them with a wide-reaching audience of businesses actively seeking their next office. The platform features advanced search filters, interactive maps, virtual tours, and detailed property information to help businesses make informed decisions.

Built with Laravel framework, this comprehensive property dealing platform streamlines the entire process of finding, comparing, and securing commercial workspace, making it easier than ever for businesses to find their ideal location.

Technical Implementation

Sub800 is built on Laravel 10 framework with a MySQL database. The platform integrates with mapping APIs for location services and utilizes Bootstrap 5 and Tailwind CSS for a modern, responsive frontend.
